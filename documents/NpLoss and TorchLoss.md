# NpLoss and TorchLoss Document
## Descriopion
Loss function supporting NumPy and Pytorch.

## Methods
* MAE(real, predict)
  * mean absulate error.
* MAPE(real, predict)
  * mean absolute percentage error.
* MSE(real, predict)
  * mean square error.
* RMSE(real, predict)
  * root mean square error.
* SNR(real, predict)
  * signal-to-noise ratio.
* SSIM(real, predict)
  * structural similarity index.
